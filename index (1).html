    How It Works

    Simple steps for volunteers and NGOs to drive real environmental impact.

    For Volunteers

    01

    Find an Event

    Browse verified environmental events by location, cause, or NGO near you.

    02

    Attend & Contribute

    03

    Earn Green Credits

    Every hour of service earns Green Credits. Redeem for rewards or donate them.

    01
