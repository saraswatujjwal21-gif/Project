🌱 Overview

Karuuna Technologies Private Limited ("Karuuna", "we", "us", or "our") operates the Karuuna platform, which connects volunteers with verified NGOs for environmental events across India. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information when you use our website, mobile app, or any related services.

Our promise: We collect only what we need, we never sell your data, and we give you full control over your information. Your trust is the foundation of everything we build.

By using Karuuna, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you have questions at any point, our privacy team is reachable at privacy@karuuna.in.

📋 Data We Collect

We collect information in three ways — what you give us directly, what is generated through your use of the platform, and (in limited cases) what we receive from third parties.

Information you provide:

  • Account details — name, email address, mobile number, city, and profile photo when you register.
  • Event registrations — your event attendance records and any dietary or accessibility preferences you share with us.
  • Communication — messages you send to our support team or NGO partners via the platform.
  • Feedback — survey responses, ratings, and post-event reviews.

Information generated automatically:

  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, search queries, and session duration.
  • Device data — browser type, operating system, IP address, and device identifiers.
  • Location — approximate city-level location inferred from your IP address (we do not access GPS without explicit permission).
  • Green Credits ledger — a record of events attended and credits earned.

Information from third parties:

  • If you sign in via Google or other OAuth providers, we receive your name and email as shared by that provider.
  • NGO partners may confirm your attendance at events, which is recorded in your profile.

⚙️ How We Use Your Information

We use your data for clearly defined purposes. We never use it in ways you wouldn't reasonably expect.

  • Providing the service — creating your account, registering you for events, issuing Green Credits, and personalising your event feed.
  • Communication — sending event reminders, registration confirmations, and important platform updates.
  • Safety & verification — confirming volunteer identities to ensure a safe environment at events.
  • Improving Karuuna — analysing aggregated, anonymised usage patterns to improve features and fix bugs.
  • Legal compliance — meeting our obligations under applicable Indian law, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

We never use your data for: targeted advertising, profiling for political purposes, or sharing with data brokers.

🤝 Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to anyone. We share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • NGO partners — when you register for an event, your name and contact details are shared with the organising NGO for attendance management. These partners are contractually bound to use your data only for this purpose.
  • Service providers — we use trusted third-party services (cloud hosting, email delivery, analytics) who process data solely on our behalf under strict data processing agreements.
  • Legal obligations — we may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business transfers — in the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred. We will notify you and you will have the option to delete your account before any transfer occurs.
  • With your consent — for any other purpose, we will ask you explicitly before sharing.

🍪 Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember your preferences (like dark mode), and understand how the platform is used.

  • Essential cookies — required for the platform to function. Cannot be disabled.
  • Preference cookies — remember your theme, language, and filter selections.
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand which features are most useful (using privacy-respecting, cookieless analytics where possible).

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling non-essential cookies will not affect your ability to use Karuuna.

🗓️ Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy.

  • Active accounts — retained for the duration of your account plus 90 days after deletion.
  • Event records — attendance logs are retained for up to 3 years for legal and audit purposes, then permanently deleted.
  • Anonymised analytics — may be retained indefinitely as they contain no personal identifiers.

You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time. See Your Rights below.

⚖️ Your Rights

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, and applicable regulations, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure — request deletion of your account and personal data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — withdraw consent for optional processing at any time, without affecting prior lawful processing.
  • Right to Grievance Redressal — raise a complaint with our Data Protection Officer or the Data Protection Board of India.

To exercise any right, email privacy@karuuna.in with your registered email address. We will respond within 15 business days as required by law.

🔒 Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with a minimum cost factor of 12.
  • Access to production databases is restricted to authorised personnel via multi-factor authentication.
  • We conduct regular security audits and penetration testing.
  • Our infrastructure is hosted on ISO 27001-certified cloud providers.

No system is completely infallible. In the event of a data breach affecting your rights, we will notify you within 72 hours of becoming aware of it, as required by law.

👶 Children's Privacy

Karuuna is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@karuuna.in and we will delete the information promptly.

📣 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
  • Send an in-app notification and email to all registered users at least 14 days before the change takes effect.
  • Maintain an archive of previous policy versions on this page.

Continued use of Karuuna after the effective date of changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
