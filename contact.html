FAQ

Quick answers

Most questions are answered here. Can't find yours? Use the form above.

How do I register as a volunteer on Karuuna?

Simply create a free account using your email or Google sign-in, complete your profile with your city, and you're ready to browse and register for eco-events near you. Registration takes under 2 minutes.

How does the Green Credits system work?

Each event carries a Green Credits value based on its duration, impact category, and effort level. Credits are automatically added to your account when the organising NGO confirms your attendance. You can redeem credits with our partner brands or donate them to conservation funds.

How are NGOs verified on the platform?

Every NGO goes through a manual vetting process: we verify their registration under the Societies Registration Act or Companies Act, review their past impact reports, and conduct a video call with their leadership team. Only approved organisations can list events on Karuuna.

Can corporates use Karuuna for employee volunteering?

Yes! We have a dedicated CSR dashboard that lets HR teams schedule group volunteering days, track participation, and generate impact reports for CSR compliance. Email csr@karuuna.in to get started or book a demo call.

Is Karuuna available outside major cities?

We currently cover 58 cities across India and are rapidly expanding. If your city isn't listed, you can sign up as a City Captain to help us launch in your area — email hello@karuuna.in with the subject "City Captain".

How do I report a problem or a fraudulent listing?

Use the "Report" button on any event or NGO page, or email hello@karuuna.in with details. Our trust and safety team reviews all reports within 48 hours. For urgent safety concerns, call our helpline directly.
