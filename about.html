Our Problem

Turning care into climate action

Environmental challenges such as pollution, climate change, deforestation, and waste mismanagement are increasing at an alarming rate. Although many NGOs are actively working to address these issues, they often face difficulties in reaching the right audience, gaining visibility for their initiatives, and coordinating with volunteers effectively. At the same time, many individuals are willing to contribute towards environmental causes but struggle to find trustworthy and accessible opportunities. This disconnect between NGOs and potential volunteers results in reduced participation and limits the overall impact of environmental efforts.

Our Solution

A carbon-aware generation

Karunna is a centralized digital platform designed to bridge the gap between NGOs and volunteers. It enables NGOs to easily post and manage their environmental events while allowing volunteers to discover and participate in activities based on their interests and availability. By providing a streamlined system for communication, event management, and engagement, Karunna simplifies the process of collaboration. This ensures that motivated individuals can take meaningful action, helping NGOs expand their reach and collectively contribute towards a more sustainable and greener future.
